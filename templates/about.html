globesuggest was built for one reason: to give Indian manufacturers, exporters, and D2C founders an unfair advantage in Google and AI rankings.

Our Mission

Unlike traditional SEO agencies that sell vague "strategy," globesuggest turns SEO/AEO into a repeatable system. Every product page you generate follows a proven structure designed to rank on Google Search, AI Overviews, and future AI recommendation models without guesswork, without agencies, without ₹50,000/month retainers.

Our philosophy is simple: If a product deserves visibility, its digital footprint should be impossible to ignore.

globesuggest is a part of the 1Matrix ecosystem and is engineered specifically for:

  • Indian MSMEs
  • Manufacturers & B2B suppliers
  • Exporters targeting global buyers
  • D2C founders building their first scalable product page

We replaced manual SEO with a full-stack AI-ranking engine that assembles landing pages, schema, content, image alt data, internal linking, buyer-intent keywords, and AEO-structured information automatically.

Our Story

Before globesuggest, Indian manufacturers depended on marketplaces like Amazon, Flipkart, and IndiaMART while ignoring Google, the biggest untapped source of high-intent buyers. Directories produced weak leads, and SEO agencies delivered slow, inconsistent, and outdated work.

So we built the opposite.

globesuggest began as a 1Matrix internal ranking framework built for speed, buyer intent depth, and AI readability. After testing it across multiple industries—essential oils, packaging, handicrafts, ingredients, industrial parts—the result was obvious:

When product data is structured correctly, Google and AI automatically push it up.

Today globesuggest creates Google-first product pages that act like mini authority hubs, capturing buyers searching for exact SKUs.
